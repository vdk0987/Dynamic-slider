Taj Mahal
This mausoleum complex in Agra, India, is regarded as one of the world’s most iconic monuments and is perhaps the finest example of Mughal architecture.
Colosseum
The Colosseum in Rome was built in the first century by order of the Emperor Vespasian.
Christ the Redeemer
Christ the Redeemer, a colossal statue of Jesus, stands atop Mount Corcovado in Rio de Janeiro.
Great Wall of China
One of the world’s largest building-construction projects, the Great Wall of China is widely thought to be about 8,850 km long.
